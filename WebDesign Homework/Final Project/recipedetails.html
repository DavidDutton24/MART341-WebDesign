Quinoa Salad

A light, refreshing salad with quinoa, fresh veggies, and a tangy lemon dressing. Perfect for a healthy lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa thoroughly in cold water.
  2. In a medium pot, combine quinoa and water. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce heat. Simmer for 15 minutes or until the quinoa is cooked and water is absorbed.
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  4. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and parsley.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
