How to Make Homemade Pizza

Follow this step-by-step guide to create your own pizza from scratch.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, yeast, water, and olive oil.
  2. Let the dough rise for at least 1 hour.
  3. Roll out the dough and add your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 475Â°F for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and enjoy your homemade pizza!
